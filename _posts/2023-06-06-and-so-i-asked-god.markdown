And so I looked at the pill bottle sadly, asking through the Our Father how to find my way out. First, he replied and said that the pill bottle is nothing more than a sophisticated potion of his elements. Then, he said to not be perturbed, as the potion is only this, nothing more. And then my nerves were calm, and the sadness turned to bliss.
