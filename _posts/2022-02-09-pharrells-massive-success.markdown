Pharrel Williams is best known for his breakout single [Happy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZbZSe6N_BXs), a wonderful song coming at the tail end of a nasty recession.

2014 is a page of my childhood. (2022-2014=8 years ago). 10 year old me remembers everyone and their mother, father, uncle and grandmother knew this song. It was crazyness. Up until about 2 weeks ago, thats all I knew about Mr. Williams. (with the exception of the song [neon guts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nTOj3UQhkLA) by lil uzi vert)

With that said, Pharrell's In My Mind is lightyears ahead of its 2006 release. The entire album feels like a 15 song affirmation that when you have a plan, you win. Skateboard P shows an inspiring amount of confidence in his vision, plan, and execution throughout the entire album.



**My nigga close your eyes. Just picture yourself just holdin' pies
Implement a plan and you surely rise. This promised by the man that controls the skies**
