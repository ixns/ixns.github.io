![In My Mind album cover](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/6/63/Pharrell_-_In_My_Mind.png)

Pharrel Williams is best known for his breakout single [Happy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZbZSe6N_BXs), a song which was SO POPULAR that it had all of America singing. This song was in the movie (Despicable Me 2)[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Despicable\_Me\_2]. That's big clout.

The year is 2014. Happy just dropped.

(2022-2014=8 years ago). 10 year old me remembers everyone and their mom, father, uncle and grandmother knew this song. Up until about 2 weeks ago, thats all I knew about Mr. Williams. (with the exception of the song [neon guts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nTOj3UQhkLA) by lil uzi vert)
With that said, Pharrell's In My Mind is lightyears ahead of its 2006 release. The entire album feels like a 15 song affirmation that when you have a plan, you win. Skateboard P shows an inspiring amount of confidence in his vision, plan, and execution throughout.

SKATEBOARD P makes it very clear that even though his upbringing was fraught with darkness:
**Imagine ten years old, full of doubt and defeat
Growing up around criminals, with clout and deceit**
[Best Friend](https://genius.com/Pharrell-williams-best-friend-lyrics)

and the death of his grandmother who inspired his music to begin with:
**My grandma Loucelle used to tell me, "You about what you weep"
She used to help me with my homework, addition, subtraction
Added faith to my life and doubt got subtracted
Wanna skip your mind from crying? Better learn something, son
You be beatin' on my couches, why don't you try hit the drums?
Look, ooh, you see me, Ma, they wish they could be me, Ma
As I got better, her body was eaten by leukaemia
Seventh grade, it was cursed and sad
But the gift within it was when I first met Chad
But even Chad could tell you that my Christmas was jinxed
'Cause Grandma Loucelle died on 12th 19th**
[Best Friend](https://genius.com/Pharrell-williams-best-friend-lyrics)


He was able to find his way through that darkness, and build his vision for himself:
**My nigga close your eyes. Just picture yourself just holdin' pies
Implement a plan and you surely rise. This promised by the man that controls the skies**
[How does it feel](https://genius.com/Pharrell-williams-how-does-it-feel-lyrics)

P comes back again again to this idea of "vision", wiring together a plan and practicing it:
**It's incredible how I etch my plans out
To be physically subtle and financially stand out**
[Show you how to hustle](https://genius.com/Pharrell-williams-show-you-how-to-hustle-lyrics)


**Whoever said anything worth working for would be painless?**
[Best Friend](https://genius.com/Pharrell-williams-best-friend-lyrics)


And, my favorite track on the album: [You can do it too](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8B0R2sDJlL4). You really can do it too, is basically what Pharrell is saying here.


**Go listen to the full album.**
